WA Backup

Automatic WhatsApp backup to Google Drive for Android

Download APK View on GitHub

Features

  • Manual & automatic scheduled backups (daily/weekly)
  • WhatsApp Business & Personal support
  • Chunked resumable uploads to Google Drive
  • Real-time progress notifications
  • Backup retention management
  • WiFi-only mode
  • Root mode for direct database access
  • Retry with exponential backoff

Quick Start

1. Download the APK from the link above
2. Install on your Android device
3. Follow the setup wizard — sign in to Google and grant storage access
4. Tap Backup Now or enable automatic backups

Privacy & Terms

WA Backup does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device and your Google Drive.
